What's New

  • All Safety Measures Against Covid-19

    While in the lockdown for pandemic situation, Covid-19, Baldwin's Sopia have been upgrading their campuses. They are taking all possible security measures to prevent it from infecting any student, teacher or any of the staff. The staff have been trained to learn how to santitize the rooms, clean the buses, sweep the gardens and so on. They are measuring distancing between desks and taping red crosses on the seats which did not maintain distancing. All the activity class rooms were expanded and webcams were placed in every class, so that children in the online batch (since they cannot let everybody in at once) don't miss out on what the physical school batch are doing.

    • New learnings, New tools

      In Covid-19 lockdown, our teachers know the old board and marker won't do the trick. They alsoo know that google meet, zoom and other places are not as safe secure and easy to use Microsoft Teams is. The school has given us E-mail addresses which only other Baldwin's E-mail addresses can contact, keeping us safe and secure. They have created MS Teams accounts for us using those E-mails so the easy to use MS Teams is also secure. Our teachers have been provided with multiple different tools like Microsoft whiteboard and Classroomscreen that help them teach. All of them have purposes, and even though everything has to be online now, our schooling has not lost its flair of ma'am presenting her whiteboard (offline or online), we all also wanting to present (our screen or write on the board), and such other habits.
